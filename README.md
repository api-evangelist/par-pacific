# Par Pacific Holdings (par-pacific)
Par Pacific Holdings is an entrepreneurial liquid fuels company focused on owning and operating energy and infrastructure businesses in logistically-complex markets. The company operates refining, retail, and logistics businesses across Hawaii, Washington, Wyoming, and Montana, with retail brands including 76, Hele, and nomnom.
